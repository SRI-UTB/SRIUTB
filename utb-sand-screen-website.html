About Our Lab

At the forefront of sand control technology for the energy sector.

Our Mission

The Sand Screen Retention Innovation Lab at the University of Technology Brunei (UTB) is dedicated to developing and testing advanced sand control technologies for oil and gas production applications.

We combine academic research with practical industry solutions to address the challenges of sand production in wells, which can cause erosion, plugging, and equipment failures.

Our Expertise

Our multidisciplinary team specializes in:

  • Advanced sand screen design and testing
  • Computational fluid dynamics for sand transport
  • Novel materials for sand control applications
  • Field implementation strategies
  • Performance evaluation and optimization

Our Research

Advancing the science and technology of sand control

Novel Screen Geometries

Investigating innovative screen designs that maximize retention efficiency while minimizing pressure drop.

Smart Materials for Sand Control

Developing responsive materials that adapt to changing well conditions for enhanced sand control performance.

Multi-phase Flow Optimization

Understanding and optimizing sand screen performance in complex multi-phase flow environments.

Sustainable Sand Management

Exploring environmentally friendly approaches to sand management in oil and gas production.
